About

I am Muhammad Dicky Afriza, born in Semarang on August 6 2001, currently I am studying in the Department of Informatics Engineering, Dian Nuswantoro University Semarang. Due to the impact of the COVID-19 outbreak, I returned to my village to be precise in Kendal, lectures were also conducted online, but I still tried to study productively by participating in a program from an organization called Griya Peradaban, namely alternative lectures.

Even though I grew up in a simple family, my parents always motivate and encourage me in any case. Besides lectures, sometimes I take the time to help my relatives/friends in light tasks or fixing problems on computers/laptops.
